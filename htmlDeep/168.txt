<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 21pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">调剂分数线要求：初试总分和单科成绩均达到我校上述相应学科专业进入复试最低分数要求的考生，可通过“中国研究生招生信息网调剂系统”（以下简称调剂系统）填报志愿，申请调剂我校。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 0px; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">（一）调剂注意事项</span></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 17.95pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">1</span></span></span><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">、调剂考生应符合调入专业的报考条件。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 17.95pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">2</span></span></span><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">、调入专业与第一志愿报考专业相同或相近。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 17.95pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">3</span></span></span><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">、初试科目应与调入专业初试科目相同或相近，其中统考科目原则上应相同。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 17.95pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">4</span></span></span><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">、我校学术型专业原则上不接受同等学历调剂考生。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 0px; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">（二）调剂流程</span></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 21pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">按教育部要求，考生调剂必须通过国家研招网调剂系统，否则调剂无效</span></span></span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">。</span></span></span><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">调剂系统开通时间为3</span>月17</span>日-4</span>月30日，请有意向调剂到我校的考生及时填报。</p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 21pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">考生填报调剂志愿后，我校将根据考生的综合条件进行筛选，并在3</span>天内向符合条件的考生发送复试通知，复试通知以短信方式发送到考生报名时注册的手机上，请考生务必保持手机的畅通，并在通知规定的时间内登陆调剂系统确认“是否同意参加复试”，逾期不确认者将取消复试资格！</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 21.6px; text-indent: 0px; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">三、复试时间</span></span></span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 21pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">我校第一批复试将在<strong>3</strong></span><strong>月底</strong>前完成，具体复试时间、报到地点、复试流程、复试实施方案，将陆续在研究生院主页公布或以学院电话通知为准。第一批拟录取后，如仍有缺额，将依情况组织第二批复试。</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 21pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">复试内容包括专业课笔试、综合测试（包括英语听说能力）等部分。专业课笔试科目待复试报到后，到各学院进行选择确认。</span></span></span></p>

<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 21pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">第一志愿报考工商管理、公共管理、工程管理、旅游管理、会计、图书情报、审计专业学位的考生，且初试总分和单科成绩均达到国家B</span>类线要求，可申请调剂到我校的工商管理硕士（MBA</span>），但需符合工作年限要求，即：大学本科毕业后有3</span>年或3年以上工作经验的人员；获得国家承认的高职高专毕业学历后，有5年或5年以上工作经验，达到与大学本科毕业同等学力的人员；已获得硕士学位并有2年或2年以上工作经验的人员。</p>

<p style="text-align: left; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); text-transform: none; line-height: 20pt; text-indent: 21pt; letter-spacing: normal; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; word-spacing: 0px; white-space: normal; -ms-word-break: break-all; orphans: 2; widows: 2; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: rgb(102, 102, 102);"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 9pt;">第一志愿报考工商管理、公共管理、工程管理、旅游管理、会计、图书情报、审计专业学位的考生，且初试总分和单科成绩均达到国家B</span>类线要求，可申请调剂到我校的工商管理硕士（MBA</span>），但需符合工作年限要求，即：大学本科毕业后有3</span>年或3年以上工作经验的人员；获得国家承认的高职高专毕业学历后，有5年或5年以上工作经验，达到与大学本科毕业同等学力的人员；已获得硕士学位并有2年或2年以上工作经验的人员。</p>
